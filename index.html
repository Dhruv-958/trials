MCQ Test Rules

  • 1. This is a multiple-choice question (MCQ) test.
  • 2. The test consists of a set of questions with multiple choices.
  • 3. Each question has only one correct answer.
  • 4. You must choose the correct option for each question.
  • 5. Do not leave any question unanswered.
  • 6. Once you submit the test, you cannot go back and change your answers.
  • 7. You will receive your score at the end of the test.
